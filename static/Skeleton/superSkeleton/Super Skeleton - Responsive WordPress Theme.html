Flexible Grid

SuperSkeleton is more than a template, it's a comprehensive framework; A collection of several incredibly powerful scripts and CSS structures that's been tied together into a kit that's perfect as a jumping off point of your next project.

Responsive & Bulletproof

SuperSkeleton is responsive (try resizing your browser)... that means that it'll flex to fit massive screens, or itty bitty iPhone screens (and everything in between). Touch gestures even work on the slider!

Minimalist Styling

SuperSkeleton is well organized. Take what you love, leave what you don't, customize the rest. It's completely in your hands - turn it into the next big thing, or just as your little personal projects, it's up to you.


About

The SuperSkeleton layout has been designed with responsive, future-proof websites in mind.

It supports all of the content you can dream up, including image galleries, advertisement units, all function HTML elements, and lots lots more!

If you’re looking for a template to start building your own next site with, this is a great place to start!
